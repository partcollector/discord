Get to Know Us

Starting in 2005 as eBay Sellers, we supplied spare parts for motorcycles. Later, we increased our product range and started offering auto, marine, and truck vehicle parts. This is when we changed the name of our company to Part Collector.

Located in Petrich, Bulgaria, we serve thousands of customers worldwide. Within our online store, www.partcollector.org, you can find spare parts for multiple models dating back to 1970.

Today, we decided to offer any private or business the opportunity to sell or buy parts across the world from our Discord Server Platform. We are in position to introduce a new low-cost, efficient, and stable trade platform for everyone's needs.
